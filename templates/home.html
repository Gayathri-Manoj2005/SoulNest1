Essential Pet Care Tips

Regular Vet Checkups

Have routine health checks yearly (twice for seniors) and stay updated on vaccines, deworming, tick & flea care.

Balanced Food & Fresh Water

Feed species-appropriate food and ensure fresh water is available at all times to keep pets healthy & hydrated.

Daily Exercise & Play

Engage in daily walks, play sessions and training games to prevent obesity, boredom and behavioural issues.

Safe & Clean Home

Provide a clean resting area, avoid toxic items, maintain hygiene, and use ID tags or microchips for safety.
